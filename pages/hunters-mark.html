Hunter's Mark (Modified)

Hunter's Mark
1st-level divination
Casting time:1 bonus action
Range:90 feet
Components:V
Duration:Concentration, up to 1 hour
While the spell is active, you automatically mark any hostile creature you can see, improving your ability to track them if they try to escape. Until the spell ends, you deal an extra 1d6 damage to a marked target whenever you hit them with a weapon attack.
