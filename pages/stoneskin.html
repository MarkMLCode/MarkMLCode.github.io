Stoneskin (modified)

Stoneskin
4th-level abjuration
Casting time:1 action
Range:Touch
Components:V, S, M (diamond dust)
Duration:Concentration, up to 1 hour
You transform a willing creature's skin into a protective layer of living stone. For the duration of the spell, the creature gains resistance to all nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.
