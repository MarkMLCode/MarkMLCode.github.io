Hex (Modified)

Hex
1st-level enchantment
Casting time:1 bonus action
Range:90 feet
Target:A creature you can see within range
Components:V, S, M (the petrified eye of a newt)
Duration:Concentration, up to 1 hour
Classes:Warlock
You place a curse on a creature that you can see within range. Until the spell ends, anyone who hits the target with an attack deals an extra 1d6 necrotic damage to it.
