Aid (Modified)

2nd-level abjuration
Casting time:1 action
Range:30 feet
Target:You and up to 4 allies
Components:V, S, M (a tiny strip of white cloth)
Duration:8 hours
Classes:Artificer, Bard, Cleric, Paladin, Ranger
Your spell bolsters you and your allies with toughness and resolve. Each target's hit point maximum and current hit points increase by 5 for the duration.

Aid is automatically applied to up to 4 of your allies at the start of each battle.

At Higher Levels: When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, a target's hit points increase by an additional 5 for each slot level above 2nd.
