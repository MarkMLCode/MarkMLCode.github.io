Ensnaring Strike (Modified) (C)

1st-level conjuration
Casting time:1 bonus action
Range:Self
Components:V
Duration:Concentration, up to 1 minute
Classes:Ranger
A writhing mass of thorny vines appears on the target, they must succeed on a Strength saving throw or be restrained by the magical vines until the spell ends. If the target succeeds on the save, the vines shrivel away.

While restrained by this spell, the target takes 1d6 piercing damage at the start of each of its turns. A creature restrained by the vines or one that can touch the creature can use its action to make a Strength check against your spell save DC. On a success, the target is freed.

At Higher Levels: If you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 1st.
