Sleep (Modified)

Sleep
1st-level enchantment
Casting time:1 action
Range:90 feet
Target:Creatures within 20 feet of a point you choose
Components:V, S, M (a pinch of fine sand, rose petals, or a cricket)
Duration:1 minute
Classes:Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard
This spell sends creatures into a magical slumber. Each creature within 20 feet of a point you choose within range must make a Wisdom saving throw or fall unconscious for the duration.

The spell ends for an affected creature if the creature takes damage, or if someone uses an action to shake or slap the sleeper awake.

Undead and creatures immune to being charmed aren't affected by this spell.
