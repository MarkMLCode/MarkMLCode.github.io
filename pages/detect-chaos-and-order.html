Detect Chaos and Order (homebrew)

Detect Chaos and Order
1st-level divination
Casting time:1 action
Range:Self
Target:Self
Components:V, S
Duration:Up to 10 minutes
Classes:Cleric, Paladin
For the duration, you know if there is a creature with a chaotic or lawful nature within 30 feet of you, as well as where the creature is located. Specifically, you can detect the presence of any creature strongly aligned with chaos (such as demons, slaadi, and creatures of pure disorder) or with order (such as modrons, inevitable constructs, and creatures bound to strict cosmic law).

Similarly, you know if there is a place or object within 30 feet of you that has been magically imbued with chaotic or lawful energy, such as an area of entropic magic or a site consecrated to a lawful deity or force.

The spell can penetrate most barriers, but it is blocked by 1 foot of stone, 1 inch of common metal, a thin sheet of lead, or 3 feet of wood or dirt.
